About

The Beech began in 2005 as a collaborative effort between Louisville Metro Housing and Ballard High School. It grew from the need to provide a place for study and learning within the Beecher Terrace Public Housing area and to provide a place for parents/guardians to conferance with educators concerning their child’s progress. The Beech has been an avenue for students to recover core content and life skills courses.
